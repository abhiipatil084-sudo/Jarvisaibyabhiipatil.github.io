About Jarvis

Jarvis is an offline AI assistant developed by Abhii Patil as a personal project. It demonstrates how artificial intelligence can be implemented directly in the browser without requiring server connections or complex APIs.

The AI uses a knowledge database combined with natural language processing to understand and respond to user queries. All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring privacy and speed.
